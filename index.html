Nakwon Rim

Hi! I am a doctoral student in the Integrative Neuroscience program (Department of Psychology) at the University of Chicago. I mainly work with Professor Marc G. Berman at the Environmental Neuroscience Lab. I am also doing work with Knowledge Lab directed by Professor James A. Evans.

I am broadly interested in how we perceive and interact with the physical and social environment surrounding us and how the environment surrounding us affects our behavior and brain. I am also interested in incorporating large-scale data (from both experiments and observational data) and computational models into research in psychology, cognitive science, and neuroscience.
